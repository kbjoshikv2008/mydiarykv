🎯 Learning Objective 1
➕ Adding Tenths
📌 Objective: Add decimal numbers involving tenths by combining whole units and tenths separately.
🔸 Sonu’s lower arm = 2.7 units    Upper arm = 3.6 units.
What is the total length of Sonu’s arm? (in units)
🎯 Learning Objective 2
📄 Hundredths Precision
📌 Objective: Measure and compute using hundredths (0.01) by dividing a tenth into 10 smaller parts.
✨ A sheet of paper is 8.9 units long. It is folded exactly in half (lengthwise).
What is the length of the folded paper? (in units, decimal)

🐟 Add & Subtract Decimals
