Height Arrangement Explorer

Arrange the children to match the target sequence!

How It Works

Seven children are standing in a line. Each child calls out the number of children in front of them who are taller than them.

Children are numbered by height from 1 (shortest) to 7 (tallest).

Your task is to arrange the children by height so that their called-out numbers match the target sequence.

Instructions:

1. Select a target sequence from the buttons below

2. Drag and drop the children to rearrange them

3. Click "Check Arrangement" to verify your solution

4. Use "Show Solution" if you need help
