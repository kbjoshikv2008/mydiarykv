📐 Angle & Radian Measure

Angle: rotation from initial side. Positive = anticlockwise, Negative = clockwise. Measure in degrees or radians.

\[ \text{Radian} = \frac{\pi}{180}\times \text{Degree}, \quad \text{Degree} = \frac{180}{\pi}\times \text{Radian} \] \[ \pi \text{ rad} = 180^\circ,\quad 1 \text{ rad} \approx 57^\circ 16' \]

📚 Sum-to-Product & Product-to-Sum

\[ \sin x + \sin y = 2\sin\frac{x+y}{2}\cos\frac{x-y}{2},\quad \cos x + \cos y = 2\cos\frac{x+y}{2}\cos\frac{x-y}{2} \] \[ \cos x - \cos y = -2\sin\frac{x+y}{2}\sin\frac{x-y}{2},\quad \sin x - \sin y = 2\cos\frac{x+y}{2}\sin\frac{x-y}{2} \]

📖 Complete Trigonometric Identities Reference

Reciprocal
\(\csc x = 1/\sin x\)
\(\sec x = 1/\cos x\)
\(\cot x = 1/\tan x\)
Pythagorean
\(\sin^2x+\cos^2x=1\)
\(1+\tan^2x=\sec^2x\)
\(1+\cot^2x=\csc^2x\)
Negative angles
\(\sin(-x)=-\sin x\)
\(\cos(-x)=\cos x\)
\(\tan(-x)=-\tan x\)
Periodicity
\(\sin(x+2\pi)=\sin x\)
\(\cos(x+2\pi)=\cos x\)
\(\tan(x+\pi)=\tan x\)
Half-angle: \(\sin^2\frac{x}{2}=\frac{1-\cos x}{2},\quad \cos^2\frac{x}{2}=\frac{1+\cos x}{2},\quad \tan\frac{x}{2}=\frac{1-\cos x}{\sin x}=\frac{\sin x}{1+\cos x}\)
