⚖️ B. Compare & Arrange (Reasoning)

📊 Ascending order of values:

\(67 - 19\) \(67 - 20\) \(35 + 25\) \(5 \times 11\) \(120 \div 3\)

💡 Values: 48, 47, 60, 55, 40 → letters: a,b,c,d,e

🔍 Which is greater?
\(1023 + 125\) or \(1022 + 128\)

🧠 Marble reasoning: Raja had 1 more but Joy got 3 more → Joy has more.

🧮 C. Evaluate (Terms → ×/÷ before +/−)

\(28 - 7 + 8\)

\(39 - 2 \times 6 + 11\)

\(48 - 10 \times 2 + 16 \div 2\)

\(6 \times 3 - 4 \times 8 \times 5\)

⭐ Multiply/divide first, then add/subtract. For last: \(6\times3=18\), \(4\times8\times5=160\), \(18-160=-142\).

🔓 D. Remove brackets (Rewrite without brackets)

\(14 + (12 + 10)\)

\(14 - (12 + 10)\)

\(14 - (12 - 10)\)

\(500 - (250 - 100)\)

✏️ Minus before bracket flips signs: \(-(a+b) = -a-b\); \(-(a-b) = -a+b\).

🔁 E. Distributive Property – Fill the blanks

\(3 \times (6 + 7) = 3\times6 + 3\times \_\_\)

\((8 + 3)\times 4 = 8\times4 + \_\_ \times 4\)

\(5 \times (9 - 2) = 5\times9 - 5\times \_\_\)

\((15 - \_\_)\times 7 = 6 \times 7\)

📖 F. Word Problems – Expression & value

👑 Queen Alia gave 100 gold coins each to Elsa and Anna. Elsa doubled her coins, Anna has only half left. Write an expression for total coins together.

🚇 Metro ticket: adult ₹40, child ₹20. Total cost for 4 adults and 3 children.

🥭 Rahim supplies 9 kg mangoes/day, Shyam supplies 11 kg/day. Total mangoes in a week (7 days).

📖 Melvin reads a 2‑page story every day except Tuesday & Saturday. How many stories in 8 weeks? (Select equivalent expression)
