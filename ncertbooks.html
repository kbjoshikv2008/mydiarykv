Class 7 Mathematics

New Syllabus as per NEP 2020 & NCF-SE 2023
गणित प्रकाश (Ganita Prakash)

Chapter-wise Content

Chapter 1
Large Numbers Around Us: Introduces the world of lakhs, crores, millions, and billions.
Chapter 2
Arithmetic Expressions: Multiple operations, parentheses, and order of operations.
Chapter 3
A Peek Beyond the Point: Decimal numbers, representations, and operations.
Chapter 4
Expressions using Letter-Numbers: Introduction to variables and algebra basics.
Chapter 5
Parallel and Intersecting Lines: Geometry concepts with paper-folding exercises.
Chapter 6
Number Play: Computational thinking through puzzles, cryptarithms, and patterns.
Chapter 7
A Tale of Three Intersecting Lines: Properties of triangles and constructions.
Chapter 8
Working with Fractions: Operations on fractions, incorporating historic Indian methods.

Key Features:

  • Aligns with NEP 2020 and NCF-SE 2023 guidelines
  • Integrates Indian knowledge systems (e.g., Brahmagupta's fraction methods)
  • Includes fun puzzles, real-life math models, and cross-subject applications
  • Practical paper-folding geometry tools

Class 11 Mathematics

Comprehensive foundation for senior secondary level

Chapter-wise Content

Chapter 1: Sets
Introduction to sets, operations, Venn diagrams and subsets.
Chapter 2: Relations & Functions
Cartesian products, domains, co-domains, range, and standard function graphs.
Chapter 3: Trigonometric Functions
Angles, trigonometric equations, identities, and graphical functions.
Chapter 4: Induction
Principle of Mathematical Induction as a method of proof.
Chapter 5: Complex Numbers
Algebra of complex numbers, Argand plane, and quadratic equations.
Chapter 6: Linear Inequalities
Algebraic and graphical solutions of linear inequalities.
Chapter 7: Permutations & Combinations
Fundamental principles of counting, permutations, combinations.
Chapter 8: Binomial Theorem
History, statement, and proof of Binomial Theorem for positive integers.
Chapter 9: Sequences & Series
AP, GP, arithmetic/geometric mean and sum to infinity of GP.
Chapter 10: Straight Lines
Slope, angle between lines, distance formula, and forms of equations.
Chapter 11: Conic Sections
Equations of circles, ellipses, parabolas, and hyperbolas.
Chapter 12: 3D Geometry
Coordinates, distance formula, section formula in 3D space.
Chapter 13: Limits & Derivatives
First-principles calculus, limit rules, derivatives of polynomials.
Chapter 14: Mathematical Reasoning
Statements, connections, and validation of logical steps.
Chapter 15: Statistics
Measures of dispersion, mean deviation, variance, standard deviation.
Chapter 16: Probability
Random experiments, events, axiomatic probability rules.

Key Features:

  • Clear conceptual diagrams and graphical illustrations
  • Detailed solved examples for every topic
  • Provides solid foundation for JEE/competitive exam preparations

Class 12 Mathematics

Advanced calculus, geometry, and probability

Chapter-wise Content

Chapter 1: Relations & Functions
Equivalence relations, composition, inverse, binary operations.
Chapter 2: Inverse Trig Functions
Principal values, domain/range, graphs and algebraic properties.
Chapter 3: Matrices
Operations, types of matrices, transposes, symmetric/skew-symmetric matrices.
Chapter 4: Determinants
Properties, minors/cofactors, matrix adjoint, inverse, linear systems.
Chapter 5: Continuity
Continuity, differentiability, chain rule, implicit functions, Rolle's/MVT.
Chapter 6: App. of Derivatives
Rate of change, increasing/decreasing, tangent normals, maxima/minima.
Chapter 7: Integrals
Definite and indefinite integrals, methods of substitution, parts, partial fractions.
Chapter 8: App. of Integrals
Area evaluation under simple curves (lines, circles, parabolas).
Chapter 9: Differential Equations
Order, degree, general/particular solution, homogeneous/linear equations.
Chapter 10: Vector Algebra
Vectors, direction cosines, dot and cross products.
Chapter 11: 3D Geometry
Lines and planes in space, angle between, shortest distance formulas.
Chapter 12: Linear Programming
Optimization, constraints, graphical solution of linear programming.
Chapter 13: Probability
Conditional probability, Bayes' theorem, binomial distribution, random variables.

Key Features:

  • Advanced calculus coverage (35% marks weightage in boards)
  • Curated exercise problems for CBSE Class 12 board preparation
