Section 4: Word Problems (Pages 11-12)

9. A water tank is filled from a tap. If the tap is open for 1 hour, \(\frac{7}{10}\) of the tank gets filled.
How much of the tank is filled if the tap is open for:
(a) \(\frac{1}{3}\) hour?

(b) \(\frac{2}{3}\) hour?

(c) \(\frac{3}{4}\) hour?

(d) \(\frac{7}{10}\) hour?

(e) For the tank to be full, how long should the tap be running?
10. The government has taken \(\frac{1}{6}\) of Somu's land to build a road. She gives half of the remaining land to her daughter Krishna and \(\frac{1}{3}\) of the remaining land to her son Bora. She keeps the rest for herself.
(a) What part of the original land did Krishna get?

(b) What part of the original land did Bora get?

(c) What part of the original land did Somu keep for herself?
11. Find the area of a rectangle with sides \(3\frac{3}{4}\) ft and \(9\frac{3}{5}\) ft.

Find the distance between the first and last sapling.
