FET Timetabling

Fully Open Source (GPL)

FET is the premier free, open-source scheduling software. It features a powerful genetic algorithm that automatically solves complex constraints like teacher hours, room allocations, student divisions, and overlapping subjects.

Industry Leader (Commercial/Free Trial)
