Olympiad Syllabus Checklist:

Algebra: Polynomials, Inequalities, Complex Numbers, Equations Geometry: Triangles similarity, Circles, Trigonometry, Coordinate Geometry Number Theory: Divisibility, Congruences, Prime Numbers, Diophantine Eqns Combinatorics: Permutations & Combinations, Pigeonhole Principle

Tips for Olympiad Preparation:

Focus on Core Areas:

Number Theory, Combinatorics, Geometry, and Algebra form the major chunk of the paper.

Solve and Review:

Don't just look at answers. Try problems yourself first, then review multiple methods of solving them.

Accuracy Over Speed:

IOQM questions require deep analytical thinking. Spending time on a single problem to understand it fully pays off.
